The "Effort Remaining" Metric

  • "HarrisGoal2024" is 110% of Biden's votes in the 2020 election.
  • "HarrisMinimum2024" is the ratio of Evers 2022 votes / Evers 2018 votes * Biden's 2020 votes.
  • "Votes Committed" is voter responses on the doors. This is zero at the moment. The intent is to make this quantity update dynamically in future versions.
  • "Votes Expected" is the number of voters who voted Democratic in the last 4 of 4 elections. This is zero at the moment until we fill in the spreadsheet.
  • "Effort Remaining to Minimum" is "HarrisExpected2024" minus ("Votes Committed" + "Votes Expected").
  • "Effort Remaining to Goal" is "HarrisGoal2024" minus ("Votes Committed" + "Votes Expected").


                def ComputeEffortRequired(df, win_factor = 1.10, coefficient_2024 = .84):
                    ### fill in missing values in these columns with the mean of the others
                    df.fillna({'Biden20': df['Biden20'].mean(), 
                            'Evers18': df['Evers18'].mean(), 
                            'Evers22': df['Evers22'].mean()}, inplace=True)
                    
                    df['HarrisGoal2024'] = (win_factor * df['Biden20']).astype(int)
                    
                    try:
                        df['HarrisMinimum2024'] = (df['Evers22'] / df['Evers18'] * df['Biden20']).astype(int)
                    except:
                        print (df['Evers22'] , df['Evers18'] , df['Biden20'])
                    
                    ### actual voter commitments from door knocks
                    df['VotesCommitted'] = 0
                    
                    ### the existing higly-committed highly-motivatd voters
                    ### currently all zeros until we fill in the values on the spreadsheet
                    df['VotesExpected'] = df['IDs_4_4'].fillna(0).astype(int)
                    
                    ## compute the "effort remaining values
                    df['EffortRemainingToMinimum'] = (df['HarrisMinimum2024'] - (df['VotesCommitted'] + df['VotesExpected']))
                    df['EffortRemainingToGoal'] = df['HarrisGoal2024'] - (df['VotesCommitted'] + df['VotesExpected'])
                    
                    return df
