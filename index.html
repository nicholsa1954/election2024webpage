The "Effort Remaining" Metric

I'm trying to develop an "Effort Remaining" concept to measure our progress in a ward, city, or county.

I begin with a concept of a "win number" defined as 110% of Biden's votes in the 2020 Presidential election.

That win number will be harder to attain in some places and easier in others, so we need a concept of a "lift". The "lift" is 1 / the probability of a Democratic vote in that ward, averaged over the elections of 2012, 2014, 2016, 2018, and 2020. "Lift" should always be equal to or greater than 1.0. It acts as a coefficient which, when applied to the "win number", creates a measure of the effort necessary (as a count of voter commitments) to achieve the win number. The "effort remaining" is that measure minus Biden's vote count in 2020.
